The Chassis List V2

I have no affiliation with any company listed.

After searching far and wide for as many REM 700 chassis options as possible the end result is the most comprehensive chassis list in the world (for Rem 700). Please allow up to 10 seconds for the information sheets to load. Depending on your device's screen size, you may have to scroll to see the entire height and width of the information sheets

Currently in order to qualify for this list, the chassis must accept Rem 700 style actions and be able to be purchased "new." However, in future revisions of the list, this restriction will be lifted.

Information:

  • All main chassis styles will be listed. For the sake of brevity similar chassis versions may not be listed.
  • Some links may point to pages that include chassis options not entirely for REM 700 actions.
  • For classification on the chassis list, the advertised chassis/ "stock" must not need "bottom metal" or action bedding.

Any additional information to help is welcome. As always, make sure to do your own research.
